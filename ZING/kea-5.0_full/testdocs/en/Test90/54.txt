The effect of the potato late blight (Phytophthora infestans) infestation[24] on the potatoes common to Ireland, known today as Irish Potatoes, was much less in other countries since a much smaller percentage of the diet of the people of England, Wales, Scotland and the rest of Europe was centred on potatoes.
